    /02

    Timeout with Bits

    A self-hosted replacement for the long-dead timeoutwithbits.com. Cheer bits in chat to time someone out. Cap your malice at three users.

    Rules

    The Conditions

    Trigger
    Use !to, !timeout, or !timeoutwithbits as the first word, then @-mention up to three users, then cheer.
    Rate
    100 bits buys 2 minutes. Linear from there.
    Minimum
    100 bits per target. Below that, nothing happens.
    Max targets
    Three per command. Bits are split evenly across all targets.
    Per-target cap
    10 minutes total per stream session. Once they hit the cap, more bits don't buy more time.
    Immunity
    VIPs cannot be timed out. Mods and the broadcaster either.
    Anonymous
    Anonymous cheerers can't initiate. Show your face.

    Field Notes

    Worked Examples

    1. !to @viewer Cheer200 → One target. 4 minutes. Clean.
    2. !to @a @b @c Cheer600 → Three targets. 200 bits each, 4 minutes apiece.
    3. !to @rival Cheer1500 → Capped at 10 min. The other 500 bits buy you bragging rights.
    4. !to @upatree Cheer1000 → Nothing. The broadcaster is immune. Aim lower.
